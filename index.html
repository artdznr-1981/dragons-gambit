📜 How to Play
  • Move — Click your piece, then click a highlighted square to move diagonally forward.
  • Capture — Jump over an opponent's piece to capture it. If you can jump, you must.
  • Multi-Jump — If another jump is available after capturing, you must continue jumping.
  • King Me — Reach the opposite end to become a King 👑 and move in all directions.
  • Win — Capture all enemy pieces or block them from moving!
